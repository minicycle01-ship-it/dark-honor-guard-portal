The Sith Order is a disciplined and immersive Roblox group built to reflect strength, hierarchy, knowledge, and control. This portal serves as its public-facing command interface: a place where visitors, recruits, and members can study the divisions of the Order, locate official resources, and understand its structure.

The Sith Order is an ancient order of Force-wielders devoted to the dark side of the Force. It is focused on Sith-themed gameplay, with ranks, training sessions, leadership structures, and organized events shaped by discipline and teamwork.
