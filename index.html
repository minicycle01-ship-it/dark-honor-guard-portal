The Dark Honor Guard

The Dark Honor Guard is charged with the absolute protection of the Emperor and His Councillors. Their duty extends to safeguarding all Sith sanctuaries, holdings, and members of the Order.

Although the Guard is foremost a defensive force, it also serves as the primary enforcer for the Order, ensuring every law and mandate set by Sith authority is upheld without exception.

The Dark Honor Guard is a specialized group of Sith tasked with the protection of all members and places belonging to the Sith Order. Admission is tryout-based, and one must attain at minimum the rank of Sith Adept to join.

Welcome to the Dark Honor Guard — the protective and enforcement arm of the Sith Order. This network exists to provide centralized access to official information, structure, and resources for personnel and approved visitors.

Recruitment

Admission to the Dark Honor Guard is tryout-based. Applicants must hold a minimum rank of Sith Adept within the Sith Order. Interested members should monitor official channels for tryout announcements and await personnel review.

Operations

The Guard maintains protection of the Emperor, His Councillors, and all Sith holdings, while enforcing the laws and mandates of the Order. Operational notices, patrol schedules, and command updates are distributed through official channels.
